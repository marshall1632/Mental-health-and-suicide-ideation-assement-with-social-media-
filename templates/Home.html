NLP techniques In recent times neural network models in NLP have shown a significant improvement in detecting suicide ideation from the implementations that are complex deep learning computing to reduce the traditional ML systems. Recurrent neural network (RNN) is implemented to outline a group of modeling. The effectiveness of the LSTM model is the most used for long-range dependency. Sawhney et al. showed the ability and strength of the C-LSTM model and compared it to the ML and DL classifiers and showed better results (Sawhney et al., 2018). Ji et al. also used LSTM and compared them to the other five ML models, and his study shows a significant benchmark for detecting suicidal ideation on Twitter and Reddit (Ji et al., 2018).

CNN with pooling layers, convolutional and nonlinear, has been shown to be applied in NLP tasks and showed significant success better than NLP traditional methods but prevents dealing with long-range communication. Kalchbrenner et al. examined the power of CNN on n-gram characteristics by testing different sentence placements (Kalchbrenner et al., 2014). Yin and Schutze introduced the unsupervised pre-training model and multichannel word embedding to upgrade the accuracy (Yin and Schutze, 2016). Morales et al. discussed the combined model of LSTM and CNN has improved the suicide risk, and the outcome was tremendous as they feature tone and test personality (Morales et al.). The combination of using RNN and CNN works as a vector and encodes the whole sequence and has the ability to get every important data sequence and is called a hybrid.
